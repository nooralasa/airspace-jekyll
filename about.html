GEM Doctoral School

The Grenoble Ecole de Management Doctoral School brings together research training at the highest level and builds upon two program areas: the Doctorate of Business Administration and the PhD. Our mission is to change lives and organizations through research. We value all knowledge contributions to people, organizations, and, ultimately, society.

The Playground - GEM's Serious Games Lab

Grenoble École de Management has emerged as a leader in Serious Games. Since 2013, the Serious Games Lab has produced games in fields like education, technology, and much more. Serious games are designed to go beyond entertainment. They are powerful tools for learning, training and engaging individuals in complex tasks. At GEM, R&D of Serious Games focused on the development of new pedagogical approaches that make learners aware of innovation and methods of creativity as well as working with strategic private sector partners on developing internal gamified tools.
